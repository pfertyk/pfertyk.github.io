Hi, I'm Paweł Fertyk

I'm a software developer, currently living in Berlin. I work mostly with Python, but I used to tamper with Java and C++. I'm a huge fan of automated tests, open source, refactoring, and removing third party services from my life.

In 2018 I've established a small game development company: Miskatonic Studio and released my first game: Intrepid.
